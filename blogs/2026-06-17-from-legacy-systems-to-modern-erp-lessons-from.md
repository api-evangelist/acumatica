Arkansas Federal Credit Union (AFCU) transitioned from an aging Microsoft Dynamics Great Plains system to Acumatica's cloud ERP, with VP/Controller Jonathan Adams describing the move as coming "25 years into the future in one leap." Reported benefits include unified corporate credit card expense management, an accounts payable team that doubled invoice disbursement frequency, eliminated data silos, and scalable growth via Acumatica's unlimited user model.
